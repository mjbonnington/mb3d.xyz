Betfair pays homage to Paul the Octopus, who correctly predicted the outcome of games during the 2010 World Cup, in this ad campaign launched ahead of the 2014 tournament in Brazil. The spot stars an octopus playing (and winning) four games of table tennis simultaneously. Betfair is a partner of ITV's World Cup coverage, and the ad was shown during the matches.
